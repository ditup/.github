## About

**ditup** is an open-source initiative building a decentralized platform where users can:

- Meet people who care about similar things.
- Keep, share, and discover ideas and issues.
- Collaborate with like-minded folks on meaningful projects.

## Technology Stack

Building on top of Universal Social Network - a decentralized network independent of companies, platforms and apps, where members own their identity and data.

- [Solid Protocol](https://solidproject.org/) is used for decentralized online identity and data storage.
- [ActivityPub](https://activitypub.rocks/) is used for communication between decentralized actors.

Read more at [solid-contrib/activitypub-interop](https://github.com/solid-contrib/activitypub-interop/).

## Features

will be built incrementally. You can contribute to this project, or you can build new applications implementing/extending these features.

- Add interests to my profile and share them with others.
- Find and connect with people who share my interests.
- Keep track of issues in my environment that I see and care about (local or global).
- Keep track of my ideas.
- Share my ideas and issues publicly or with specific people. Explore and adopt other issues and ideas.
- Organize and join local or remote events based on shared passions.
- Start or join a project, and collaborate on addressing the issues and implementing the ideas.

Each incremental version should be usable and useful on its own.

## Contributing

Let's do it together.

The project will be governed as [Sociocracy](https://www.sociocracyforall.org/).

You don't need to be a programmer to contribute.

- Design and UX
- Community and Governance
- Advocacy
- Documentation
- Testing
- Ideas and Discussion
- Architecture and Development

You can also learn new skills by contributing.

Join [matrix chat](https://matrix.to/#/#ditup:matrix.org) or a call and introduce yourself.

You can also write email to `hello at ditup dot org`.
